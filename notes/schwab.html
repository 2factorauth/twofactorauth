Charles Schwab
Info regarding Schwab's 2FA

Schwab uses either a physical token or the Symantec VIP Access smartphone app. You can use the same VIP Access token with Schwab and with other supported services, such as Fidelity.

How to Setup 2FA for Schwab

  1. Do one of the following:
    1. Obtain a physical token by calling Schwab Account Support at +1 800-435-4000.
    2. Install the Symantec VIP Access app for iPhone or the Android version.
  2. Get the Credential ID from the token or app.
  3. Login to your accout at schwab.com.
  4. In top panel, choose Service -> Security Center. There, enable Enhanced Authentication. You will need to provide the Credential ID supplied by your token or app.
  5. The next time you log in to the Schwab website with just your password, Schwab will prompt you for a tokencode.
