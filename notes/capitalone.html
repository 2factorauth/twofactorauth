Two Factor Auth (2FA)

Capital One
Info regarding Capital One 2FA.

Capital One 2FA is always enabled after initial account setup.

When you login to your account on a new device using the desktop website, mobile website, or app, you will be prompted to receive a code through a SMS text message or email on your account.

2FA Push application (SwiftID)

Multiple individuals are reporting that SwiftID was not working even after 30+ minute calls to technical support. Technical support further states that SwiftID is not always available.

The individual logging in has the ability to choose how to perform 2FA (SMS, email or SwiftID). If SwiftID cannot be enforced, is not always available and cannot be forced as the only option by the account holder, then there really is no security benefit to the account holder.
