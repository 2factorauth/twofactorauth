Two Factor Auth (2FA)

Internet.bs
Info regarding Internet.bs's 2FA.

Internet.bs supports any software app that supports TOTP tokens.

It appears there is no information about this service provided online though it is readily accessible in the "My Profile" section of the management console.

How to Setup 2FA for Internet.bs

  1. Login to your account
  2. Under My Account, click My Profile
  3. At the top of the page, click "Enable" next to "Two-step login verification"
  4. Follow the wizard to configure your 2 Factor Authentication Provider
  5. The next time you log in to the Internet.bs website it will prompt you for your token
