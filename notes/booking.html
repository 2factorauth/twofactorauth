Booking.com
Info regarding Booking.com 2FA.

Booking.com supports 2FA via SMS and email, although intial setup requires SMS.

It appears there is no information about this service provided online though it is readily accessible in the "Security" section of your account settings.

How to Setup 2FA for Booking.com

  1. Go to the Security section of your account settings
  2. Click on the "Add Two Factor Authentication" button
  3. Select region and enter mobile phone number
  4. Click on "Send code"
  5. Enter the verification code sent by SMS and click "Verify"

How to log in using 2FA for Booking.com

  1. Go to the Sign in page
  2. Enter your email address and click "Next"
  3. Enter your password and click "Sign in"
  4. Optional: If you do not have access to your mobile phone, click on "other options" and enter the mobile phone number associated to your account to receive the code by email
  5. Enter the verification code sent by SMS or email and click "Sign in"
